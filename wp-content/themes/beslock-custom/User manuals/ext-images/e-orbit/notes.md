# e-Orbit Notes

## Current reference source
- `../e-orbit.webp`

## What this reference confirms
- e-Orbit is the most feature-dense product in the set.
- The identity depends on the tall body, upper sensor cluster, front keypad, and sculpted handle.
- The fingerprint sensor is integrated into the exterior handle, not the front panel.

## Generation cautions
- Do not simplify e-Orbit into a generic smart lock.
- Camera or sensor placement drift is an immediate reject condition.
- Interior-side scenes should preserve the screen and lower thumbturn arrangement.
