# e-Orbit Visual Profile

## Reference anchor
- Current canonical reference image: `../e-orbit.webp`
- Product family: tall integrated smart lock with camera/display panel and sculpted exterior handle

## Canonical identity
- tall monolithic body with a glossy front interaction panel
- upper front area contains a three-sensor or camera cluster and a small display zone
- front face includes a numeric touch keypad beneath the upper sensor area
- exterior handle is thick, sculpted, and separate from the upper panel
- fingerprint sensor with a blue accent ring sits near the top of the exterior handle
- interior side includes a tall screen and lower thumbturn hardware

## Protected geometry
- keep the tall full-height body and strong vertical proportions
- keep the camera or sensor cluster in the upper front section
- keep the display and keypad zones distinct from the handle body
- keep the fingerprint sensor on the upper portion of the exterior handle, not on the keypad panel
- preserve the interior screen-plus-thumbturn arrangement

## Forbidden variations
- no simplification into a standard keypad lever lock
- no removal of the camera or sensor cluster
- no relocation of the fingerprint sensor to the panel face
- no knob conversion
- no separate rim-lock box architecture

## Interaction zones
- upper front sensor or camera cluster only when the task needs identification context
- front keypad area
- exterior handle fingerprint sensor
- interior display and lower thumbturn for interior-side flows

## Lighting and realism
Protect glossy black surfaces with soft, controlled reflections.
Keep realism technical and credible; do not turn e-Orbit into a cinematic smart-home prop.
