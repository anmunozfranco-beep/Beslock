# e-Prime Notes

## Current reference source
- `../e-prime.webp`

## What this reference confirms
- e-Prime is a slim long-plate lever lock.
- The blue-ring interaction point is at the lever hub, not the handle face or tip.
- The product identity depends on a glossy full-height exterior face and a lower oval insert.

## Generation cautions
- Do not drift toward the heavier e-Flex body.
- Keep the lower oval insert present in medium and wide shots.
- Treat keypad numerals as contextual only.
