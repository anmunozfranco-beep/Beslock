# Product Truth Policy

## Non-negotiable rule
The product reference in `../../ext-images/` wins over stylistic preference.

## Requirements
- match the lock family: lever, knob, rim-lock, or integrated screen lock
- preserve major proportions and panel layout
- preserve protected geometry defined in the product visual profile
- preserve finish family unless a real reference supports a variant
- reject outputs that merge traits from different products
- preserve the correct side: exterior, interior, or edge
- keep the door-edge locking mechanism consistent across the whole product package once the OEM-backed mechanism has been chosen
- never merge interior and exterior lock bodies into a single hero-like composition for manual use

## Escalation rule
If a generator cannot preserve the product identity, downgrade the image ambition:
- move from broad scene to tighter crop
- move from hybrid to schematic
- move from schematic to an even simpler technical view if needed

Do not keep forcing photorealism once product truth is already failing.
For manual imagery after the reset policy, side separation and mechanism consistency are part of product truth, not optional styling details.
