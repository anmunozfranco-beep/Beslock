# e-Flex Visual Validation

## Product-specific pass checks
- The lock remains a long-plate lever lock.
- The exterior keypad stays in the upper front section.
- The fingerprint sensor remains on the exterior handle face.
- No camera, screen, or knob traits appear.

## Slot cautions
- For keypad-led scenes, generated numerals must not carry instruction meaning.
- For fingerprint scenes, the finger must land on the handle-face sensor, not the panel.
- For app scenes, the phone screen is contextual unless replaced by a real capture.
