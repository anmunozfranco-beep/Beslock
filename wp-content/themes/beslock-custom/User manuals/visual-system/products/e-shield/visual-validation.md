# e-Shield Visual Validation

## Product-specific pass checks
- The product remains a split-body rim-lock system.
- The exterior slab stays vertical and handle-free.
- The interior housing stays separate and box-like.
- The top fingerprint sensor and keypad remain on the exterior slab.
- No lever, knob, or camera traits appear.

## Slot cautions
- Installation and latch scenes may need hybrid or schematic treatment.
- Troubleshooting scenes should not hide the exterior slab shape.
- App scenes must not let the phone erase the split-body identity.
