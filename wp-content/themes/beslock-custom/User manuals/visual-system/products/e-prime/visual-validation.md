# e-Prime Visual Validation

## Product-specific pass checks
- The product remains a slim long-plate lever lock.
- The glossy exterior face remains a dominant identity cue.
- The blue-ring interaction point stays at the lever hub.
- The lower oval insert remains present on the front body.
- No camera, screen, or knob traits appear.

## Slot cautions
- Do not let e-Prime drift into the heavier e-Flex silhouette.
- For fingerprint scenes, the hand must target the lever-hub ring.
- For keypad scenes, do not rely on generated digits as instructional proof.
