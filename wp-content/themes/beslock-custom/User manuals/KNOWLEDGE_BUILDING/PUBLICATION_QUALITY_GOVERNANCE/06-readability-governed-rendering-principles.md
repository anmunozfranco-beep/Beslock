# Readability-governed rendering principles

- The renderer applies readability rules as advisory signals at render time.
- The renderer is forbidden from silently editing knowledge-core text.
- The renderer is forbidden from silently merging or de-duplicating entities.
- The renderer is forbidden from generating prompts or images.
- The renderer surfaces every defect; resolution lives in knowledge-core.
- The renderer records the active audience profile in publication-metadata.
