# Human usability doctrine

Usability is judged at the failure edge, not the happy path.

- Every step must be observable before the next begins.
- Every failure must offer a recovery path or an explicit terminal state.
- Every escalation must reach a real channel (no orphan cues).
- Every irreversible action must be flagged immediately before it occurs.
- Operator comprehension is a render-time signal, not a post-hoc audit.
