# 00 — Lifecycle Governance Charter

## Principles
1. Knowledge evolves; lineage does not break.
2. Every artifact has a lifecycle stage; no artifact is stage-less.
3. Promotion is gated, evidenced, and reviewed; never implicit.
4. Deprecation is recorded, never silent; archival is the only terminal state.
5. Versioning is explicit on every axis; breaking changes emit impact reports.
6. Health monitoring is continuous; critical indicators have zero tolerance.
7. Human review is mandatory at verified-truth and at safety-critical promotions.
8. Knowledge debt is named, owned, and closed only by an explicit event.
9. Governance is additive and subordinate to the knowledge-core.

## Authority areas
- lifecycle-stages

- deprecation-governance

## Hard guarantees
- Modeling-only. Does not author product knowledge.
- Subordinate to `knowledge-core`. Cannot override it.
- Lineage preservation is non-negotiable.
- Silent destruction of any semantic artifact is a governance violation.
