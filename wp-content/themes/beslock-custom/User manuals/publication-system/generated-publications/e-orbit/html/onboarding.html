Wi-Fi or EZ Mode fallback pairing

Preconditions:

  • QR pairing unavailable or failed
  • compatible Wi-Fi available
  • lock reset path accessible

Steps:

  1. Open the lock Wi-Fi onboarding family.
  2. Enter the Wi-Fi details.
  3. Enter the reset-device flow.
  4. Switch the app to EZ Mode.
  5. Follow the alternative distribution or hotspot prompts.

Outcomes:

  • fallback-pairing-attempted

conf:highverifiedsurface:appwf-app-pairing-qr

Smart Life QR pairing

Preconditions:

  • app installed and signed in
  • compatible Wi-Fi available
  • lock in pairing mode

Steps:

  1. Open Smart Life and add a device from Camera&Lock.
  2. Choose the observed Wi-Fi video lock family.
  3. Select the target Wi-Fi network and enter the password.
  4. Show the generated phone QR to the lock camera.
  5. Wait for the lock prompt and confirm success in the app.

Outcomes:

  • device-visible-in-app
  • lock-network-configured

conf:highverifiedsurface:lockwf-local-onboarding

Initial local onboarding

Preconditions:

  • lock installed correctly
  • panel wakes correctly
  • primary administrator designated

Steps:

  1. Create the administrator from the user menu.
  2. Register at least one PIN.
  3. Register at least one fingerprint.
  4. Validate both credentials immediately.

Outcomes:

  • administrator-created
  • pin-registered
  • fingerprint-registered

conf:highverifiedsurface:local-deviceproc-add-administrator

Agregar usuario -> Agregar administrador

Menu path: Agregar usuario -> Agregar administrador

Steps:

  1. Wake the lock panel.
  2. Enter the menu.
  3. Choose Agregar usuario.
  4. Choose Agregar administrador.
  5. Select the credential type.
  6. Complete the enrollment.
  7. Save the result.
  8. Test access immediately.

Validation checks:

  • Access works correctly.
  • The user can enter without difficulty.
  • A backup credential exists.
