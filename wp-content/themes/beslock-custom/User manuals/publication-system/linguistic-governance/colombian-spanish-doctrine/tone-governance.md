# Tone governance

- Instructional tone is direct, professional and second-person formal: `Pulse`,   `Ingrese`, `Confirme`, `Verifique`.
- Never use first person (`vamos a…`, `te enseño…`).
- Never use marketing adjectives (`fácil`, `rápido`, `increíble`, `intuitivo`).
- Warnings are factual: state the condition, the consequence, and the operator action.   Do not soften with `por favor` or `recomendamos`.
- Troubleshooting is diagnostic and neutral: no blame language (`usted hizo mal`),   no apologies (`lamentamos…`).
