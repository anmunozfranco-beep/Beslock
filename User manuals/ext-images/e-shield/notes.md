# e-Shield Notes

## Current reference source
- `../e-shield.webp`

## What this reference confirms
- e-Shield depends on a split exterior-interior architecture.
- The exterior slab has both a top fingerprint sensor and keypad.
- The product family is neither a lever lock nor a knob lock.

## Generation cautions
- The exterior slab and interior box must stay visibly distinct.
- Do not invent a handle to make the scene feel more familiar.
- Installation or latch content may need hybrid or schematic treatment.
