# e-Flex Visual Profile

## Reference anchor
- Current canonical reference image: `../e-flex.webp`
- Product family: smart lever lock with long exterior and interior escutcheon plates

## Canonical identity
- tall rectangular body with rounded corners
- dark black finish with a matte structural body and a glossy front touch-panel area
- straight horizontal lever handles on both sides
- exterior face shows a touch keypad in the upper section
- exterior lever includes a circular fingerprint sensor with a blue accent ring on the handle face

## Protected geometry
- keep the long vertical plate proportions front and back
- keep the keypad restricted to the upper front panel
- keep the handle centered on the plate body
- keep the fingerprint sensor on the exterior handle face, not on the plate
- do not remove the simple rear controls on the interior plate

## Forbidden variations
- no knob conversion
- no camera cluster
- no face-recognition screen
- no separate exterior and interior modules
- no fingerprint sensor moved to the keypad panel

## Interaction zones
- exterior keypad touch area
- exterior handle fingerprint sensor
- handle grip and lever action zone
- interior lock controls only when the step explicitly needs them

## Lighting and realism
Use neutral documentary lighting with enough reflection control to preserve the glossy panel edges.
Do not over-style the product or turn it into a luxury hero render.
