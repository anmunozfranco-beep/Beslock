# e-Nova Visual Profile

## Reference anchor
- Current canonical reference image: `../e-nova.webp`
- Product family: round smart knob lock with circular roses

## Canonical identity
- compact knob-based form factor
- dark black finish with smooth rounded knob shells
- exterior knob face contains a centered fingerprint sensor with a blue accent ring
- interior side uses a matching round knob geometry with a thumbturn element
- no exterior keypad panel and no long escutcheon plate

## Protected geometry
- keep the circular rose-and-knob architecture on both sides
- keep the fingerprint sensor centered on the exterior knob face
- keep the product compact and symmetric as a knob family
- preserve the absence of a tall body, lever handle, or camera array

## Forbidden variations
- no lever conversion
- no front keypad panel
- no vertical escutcheon plate
- no camera, screen, or face-recognition hardware
- no separate rim-lock interior box

## Interaction zones
- exterior fingerprint touch point on the knob face
- grip and twist zone of the knob body
- interior thumbturn or inner-control zone when needed

## Lighting and realism
Use soft reflections that keep the rounded knob geometry readable.
Do not stretch e-Nova into a larger smart-lock silhouette just to make scenes easier.
