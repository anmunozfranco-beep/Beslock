# e-Touch Visual Profile

## Reference anchor
- Current canonical reference image: `../e-touch.webp`
- Product family: minimalist smart lever set with round roses and keypad markings on the exterior handle

## Canonical identity
- no long escutcheon plate, only round roses and lever bodies
- exterior handle is glossy black and carries the numeric markings directly on the handle surface
- a circular fingerprint sensor with an accent ring sits near the base of the exterior handle
- interior handle is much cleaner, with minimal visible indicators
- overall silhouette is slim, minimal, and handle-led rather than panel-led

## Protected geometry
- keep the round roses on both sides
- keep the keypad or numeric markings on the exterior handle itself
- keep the fingerprint sensor near the handle base, not on a panel or tip
- preserve the absence of a tall body, screen, or camera cluster
- keep the interior handle visually plain compared with the exterior side

## Forbidden variations
- no long escutcheon plate
- no front keypad slab
- no knob conversion
- no camera or face-recognition screen
- no fingerprint sensor moved to the handle tip or a separate panel

## Interaction zones
- exterior handle-base fingerprint sensor
- exterior handle numeral area for PIN-led interactions
- lever grip and door-opening action zone
- minimal interior controls only when required

## Lighting and realism
Use controlled reflections so the numbers on the handle read as structure cues without becoming fake textual evidence.
Do not bulk e-Touch up into a larger smart-lock body.
