# e-Touch Notes

## Current reference source
- `../e-touch.webp`

## What this reference confirms
- e-Touch is a handle-led product with no long plate.
- The numeric interaction zone is on the exterior handle surface itself.
- The fingerprint sensor sits near the base of the exterior handle.

## Generation cautions
- Do not replace the handle-mounted numerals with a separate keypad panel.
- Keep the round roses visible in hero and installed-context views.
- Treat handle numerals as structure cues, not exact instructional text.
