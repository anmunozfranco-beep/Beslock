# e-Nova Visual Validation

## Product-specific pass checks
- The lock remains a knob product with circular roses.
- The exterior fingerprint sensor stays centered on the knob face.
- No keypad panel, lever, screen, or camera appears.
- The product remains compact and rounded.

## Slot cautions
- Revalidate any standardized slot that assumes keypad interaction.
- Troubleshooting scenes must show the knob-face sensor clearly.
- App scenes must not let the phone replace the product identity.
