# Framing Rules

## Purpose
Choose framing according to documentation use, not aesthetics.

## Framing modes
- Hero: full product or installed product, enough context to identify door placement, no distracting lifestyle cues
- Interaction: tight crop around hand and interaction zone, single action, product remains identifiable
- App pairing: phone and lock both visible, phone secondary unless the article is explicitly app-led
- Troubleshooting: clear view of the failing interaction zone, neutral human gesture, no melodrama
- Schematic or hybrid: orthographic or near-orthographic view, minimal perspective distortion

## General rules
- Preserve the protected geometry from the visual profile.
- Do not crop out the feature that makes the product identifiable.
- Keep backgrounds quiet and surfaces clean.
- Prefer angles that reveal interaction zones without visually redesigning the product.
